aiy.leds¶

APIs to control certain LEDs connected to the Vision/Voice Bonnet: the RGB LED in the button and the privacy LED in the Vision Kit. These APIs are not compatible with the Voice HAT (V1 Voice Kit).

For examples, see leds_example.py.

To control LEDs attached to the bonnet’s GPIO pins or those on the Vision/Voice Bonnet board, instead use aiy.pins.
